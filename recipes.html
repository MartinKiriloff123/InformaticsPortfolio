Pizza

  • Time: 25 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
dough 0.5kg
tomato paste 1 jar
ham 12-14 slices
Cheese 0.4kg
mushrooms 0.1kg
Olives 1 cup

Step 1: Lay out the dough in a circuar shape and cover it with athin layer of tomato paste.

Step 2: Cut the mushroms and the cheese. Layer the ham, olives,mushrooms and lastly the cheese on top of the dough

Step 3: Seaso and then bake for about 15mmin on 200 degrees.

Source: My Girlfriend

Sandwich

  • Time: 10min
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Olive paste 10g
Bread 1 mini baggette
tomato two slices
ham 2/3 slices
Avocado half avocado sliced
Mozzarella Couple of slices

Step 1: |Slice the baggette in half and spread the olive paste one or both of the pieces

Step 2: Layer the rest of the ingredients on one of the bread slices and put the other one on top

Step 3: Optional: You can toast the baggette for a bit before slcing it in half.
